## **Style Guidelines for AI Generation**

**Color Palette:**
- Muted business colors (navy, grey, burnt orange, muted teal)
- Avoid overly bright/cheerful colors
- Professional but slightly cynical tone

**Style References:**
- "Editorial cartoon style like The Economist"
- "Business illustration like Harvard Business Review"
- "Infographic style like Visual Capitalist"
- "Modern flat design with subtle texture"

**Composition:**
- Clear focal point
- Visual metaphors and symbolism
- Readable at thumbnail size
- Scalable for print (vector-friendly)

**Avoid:**
- Generic motivational imagery
- Overly complex compositions
- Copyrighted characters/logos
- Offensive stereotypes

---

## **Technical Specifications**

**For Print:**
- Resolution: 3000x3000px minimum (300 DPI)
- Format: PNG with transparency or high-quality JPG
- Color space: CMYK-safe RGB

**For Web:**
- Resolution: 1920x1080px minimum
- Format: PNG or JPG optimized for web
- File size: < 500KB preferred

---

## **Prompt Engineering Tips**

1. **Be specific about style**: Always include "business illustration style" or "editorial cartoon"
2. **Specify colors**: "muted business colors, professional color palette"
3. **Include composition details**: "centered composition, clear focal point"
4. **Add quality markers**: "high quality, professional, detailed"
5. **Mention brand alignment**: "satirical tone, data-driven humor"

**Example Well-Formed Prompt:**
```
"Satirical business illustration showing overwhelmed entrepreneur drowning in
unsold dropshipping inventory boxes in warehouse setting, muted professional
color palette (navy, grey, burnt orange), editorial cartoon style like The
Economist, centered composition, high quality, detailed"
```
