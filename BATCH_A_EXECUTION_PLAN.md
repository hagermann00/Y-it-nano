## 🎯 Quality Standards

### Research Brief Standards
- Minimum 2,500 words of verified content
- 3+ credible sources per statistic
- Clear distinction between verified, conflicting, and missing data
- Executive summary with top 5 key findings
- Strategic assessment (market attractiveness score)

### Case Study Standards
- 11 distinct customer archetypes
- Full anonymization (composite characters)
- Clear failure mechanism analysis
- Realistic financial outcomes
- "What they wish they knew" insights
- 500-800 words per case study

### Affiliate Catalog Standards
- Minimum 50+ opportunities per topic
- Commission rates documented
- Cookie durations listed
- Approval requirements noted
- Ethical scoring applied
- Revenue potential ranking
