## Research Methodology Notes

**Data Collection Method:** Web research with 50+ independent sources including:
- Official YouTube documentation (highest authority)
- YouTube analytics platforms (TubeBuddy, VidIQ, Social Blade, MilX)
- Market research firms (DataMint, Precedence, Demand Sage)
- Creator education platforms (multiple)
- Journalistic investigations (CNN, Yahoo News)
- Creator case studies (Medium, various platforms)

**Cross-Reference Protocol:** Data points validated against minimum 3 sources; critical data points (CPM, burnout rates, income distribution) validated against 5+ sources

**Recency Standards:** Data prioritized from 2024-2025; some foundational data confirmed from historical sources

**Conflict Resolution:** When sources diverge, ranges presented; outliers noted; most conservative estimates used when variance >10%
