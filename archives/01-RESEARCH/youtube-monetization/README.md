- **The guru economy profiteering** where $100M-$300M annually flows to course creators instead of platform creators
- **The platform dependency risks** where YouTube policy changes can instantly eliminate income
- **The burnout epidemic** affecting 79-83% of creators

**CPM/Seasonal Variation:**
- Finance niche: $4.90 CPM (highest)
- Gaming niche: $1.50-$2.50 CPM
- January collapse: 40-45% drop from December

- Top gurus making: $50K-$600K+/month
