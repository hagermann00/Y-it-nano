## CONFLICTING DATA

### Conflict 1: Regional Market Leadership

- **Claim:** Which region leads dropshipping market
- **Source A (Grand View Research):** North America 31-33% market share
- **Source B (Precedence Research):** Asia Pacific 35-36% market share
- **Resolution:** Both regions roughly equal (~31-36% each). Difference likely due to methodology (B2B vs B2C classification, timing). Documented both perspectives as valid.

### Conflict 2: Market Size 2024

- **Claim:** Exact global market size
- **Source A (Grand View):** $365.67 billion
- **Source B (Expert Market):** $309.63 billion
- **Range:** $309-366 billion (18% variance)
- **Resolution:** Used consensus range $344-365B. Variance acceptable for market estimates.

### Conflict 3: Profit Margin Claims

- **Claim:** Average dropshipping profit margins
- **Source A (SparkShipping):** 10-25% typical
- **Source B (Dropship.io):** 20-30% typical
- **Practitioner Reports (Reddit):** 10-15% after all costs
- **Resolution:** Used conservative 10-25% range. Higher claims (20-30%) reflect gross margins before ad costs.

### Conflict 4: Time to Profitability

- **Claim:** How long to become profitable
- **Source A (Dropship.io):** 2 months for active dropshippers
- **Source B (General consensus):** 3-6 months typical
- **Source C (Conservative):** 6-12 months
- **Resolution:** Documented all ranges. "2 months" applies to top 20% with 20+ hrs/week and adequate budget. "3-6 months" is median. "6-12 months" is conservative/realistic for beginners.
