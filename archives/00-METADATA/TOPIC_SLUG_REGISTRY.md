## Naming Convention Rules

**Format:** `[lowercase]-[hyphen-separated]-[2-3-words]`

**Valid Examples:**
- ✅ dropshipping
- ✅ affiliate-marketing
- ✅ course-creation
- ✅ print-on-demand

**Invalid Examples:**
- ❌ Dropshipping (capital letter)
- ❌ dropshipping_business (underscore)
- ❌ drop shipping (space)
- ❌ dropshipping-business-model-guide (4+ words)
