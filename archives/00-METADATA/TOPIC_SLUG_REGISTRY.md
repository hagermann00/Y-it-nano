## Topic Status Definitions

- **Queued:** Topic identified, awaiting research initiation
- **Research In Progress:** Active research phase (Days 1-7)
- **Research Complete:** All research files validated and archived
- **Content In Progress:** Manuscript being written
- **Content Complete:** Manuscript final and approved
- **Design In Progress:** Designer working on layout
- **Design Complete:** KDP package ready
- **Production:** Book live on Amazon
- **Archived:** Topic complete, monitoring metrics only

## Naming Convention Rules

**Format:** `[lowercase]-[hyphen-separated]-[2-3-words]`

**Valid Examples:**
- ✅ dropshipping
- ✅ affiliate-marketing
- ✅ course-creation
- ✅ print-on-demand

**Invalid Examples:**
- ❌ Dropshipping (capital letter)
- ❌ dropshipping_business (underscore)
- ❌ drop shipping (space)
- ❌ dropshipping-business-model-guide (4+ words)
