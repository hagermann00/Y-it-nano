**Character Summary:**
28-year-old marketing coordinator who invested $2,800 in a fashion accessories dropshipping venture. Failed due to oversaturated niche and poor product selection. 240 hours invested with zero profit.

**Primary AI Prompt (for Midjourney/DALL-E):**
```

illustration with dark humor—exaggerated features but recognizable. Digital art with
hand-drawn feel and professional polish. Color palette: muted rose pinks and blacks with
ironic gold accents highlighting the failed products. Lighting emphasizes exhaustion.
```

**Visual Theme:** Fashion victim of her own ambition—surrounded by evidence of poor choices

**Emotional Tone:** Betrayal mixed with dawning realization; the moment the dream dies

**Pose & Composition:**
- Sitting among scattered inventory
- One hand holding spreadsheet (downward trending)
- One hand touching temples (headache)
- Fashion magazines arranged mockingly
- Product boxes tumbling around

**Key Visual Details:**
- Instagram filter icons malfunctioning (broken hearts, disappearing likes)
- Rose gold watch sitting uselessly nearby
- AliExpress logo barely visible on boxes
- Her expression: pursed lips, wide eyes, single tear

## 4 SATIRICAL SVG CHART SPECIFICATIONS

### CHART 1: THE SURVIVAL CURVE (Failure Cascade)

**Chart Type:** Line graph with dramatic annotations
**Data:** 100 dropshippers → 1-2 surviving after 2 years
**Primary Purpose:** Show brutal attrition and timeline of failure

**SVG Structure:**
```
Viewport: 800px × 600px
Grid: 100 dropshippers (Y-axis) × 24 months (X-axis)
```

**Data Points & Milestones:**

| Month | Survivors | Icon | Label | Animation |
|-------|-----------|------|-------|-----------|
| 0 | 100 | 🎉 | "EXCITEMENT" | Confetti drops |
| 1 | 78 | 💦 | "Reality Check" | Sweat drops fall |
| 3 | 45 | 🔥 | "PANIC MODE" | Fire animation |
| 6 | 12 | ☠️ | "Death of Dream" | Skull floats |
| 9 | 4 | 📉 | "Acceptance" | Line flattens |
| 12 | 2 | 🏆 | "Miracle Survivors" | Confetti (ironic) |
| 24 | 1 | 👑 | "The One" | Crown appears |

**Visual Style:**
- Y-axis: Green (100) → Orange (50) → Red (#E63946, near 0)
- Line: Bold 4px stroke, jagged/dramatic
- Background: Light grid (#F5F5F5)
- Milestone icons: Large (60px), emoji or custom SVG
- Text labels: Dramatic/humorous messaging

**Animation Specifications:**
- Line draws from left to right over 3 seconds (ease-in-out)
- Icons appear at milestone points with scale animation (0 → 1)
- Color gradient animates as line draws
- Labels fade in with slight bounce

**Satirical Elements:**
- Confetti on Day 0 (unrealistic optimism)
- Rapid drop-off between months 1-3 (cruel reality)
- Flat line at 1 survivor (and they're probably lucky, not skilled)
- Ironic trophy/crown for the one survivor (who probably quit anyway)

**Color Specifications:**
```
Start (Green): #2ECC71
Middle (Orange): #F39C12
End (Red): #E63946
Background: #F5F5F5
Text: #333333
```

## VISUAL GUIDELINES

### Overall Y-It Aesthetic

**Core Principles:**
- Dark humor WITHOUT depression
- Exaggerated BUT recognizable
- Satirical BUT respectful (laugh at the system, not the people)
- Professional ENOUGH for KDP, funny ENOUGH to share

**What This Means:**
- Characters are caricatured (exaggerated features) but still sympathetic
- Situations are comedic but not cruel to the individual
- The failures are the subject of mockery, not the people themselves
- Include visual metaphors and symbols (fire for chaos, gears for inefficiency, etc.)

### Style Specifications

**Medium:** Digital illustration (vector or digital painting)—NOT photograph

**Technique:**
- Vector-based character work (scalable, clean)
- Hand-drawn linework feel (human touch)
- Digital painting for depth/shading
- Hybrid: Clean lines + organic texturing

**Character Design:**
- Slightly caricatured features (not grotesque, just exaggerated)
- Modern, relatable clothing
- Age-appropriate proportions
- Diverse, realistic representation
- Expressive faces capturing specific emotions

**Composition:**
- Rule of thirds for balance
- Clear focal point (the character)
- Environmental context (supporting the narrative)
- Symbolic elements integrated naturally

**Resolution & Export:**
- Portraits: 300 DPI for print quality
- Dimensions: 2" × 2.5" (portrait orientation, standard KDP size)
- Format: PNG with transparency where needed
- Charts: SVG (scalable, animatable, web-ready)

### Lighting & Atmosphere

**Lighting Approach:**
- Dramatic, directional lighting (not flat)
- Shadows emphasizing emotion
- Color temperature matching character mood

**Atmosphere by Character:**
- Sarah: Spotlight on failure (harsh, revealing)
- Marcus: Cool, clinical lighting (tech lab aesthetic)
- David: Flickering flames, chaotic lighting
- Jennifer: Soft, sad lighting (melancholic)
- Tyler: Multiple conflicting light sources (chaos)
- Raj: Deep shadows (exhaustion, late nights)
- Patricia: Warm, dawn/sunrise lighting (hope)

## SATIRICAL TONE GUIDELINES

**DO:**
- Exaggerate features for comedic effect
- Use visual metaphors (fire, gears, money burning)
- Show emotional honesty (the pain is real, but we laugh)
- Make the system the villain, not the people
- Include ironic visual details (gold accents on failure, etc.)
- Capture the psychological journey (excitement → crash → acceptance)

**DON'T:**
- Mock or demean individuals
- Create stereotypes based on race/ethnicity (except self-aware, consensual)
- Be cruel or genuinely depressing
- Glorify failure
- Make fun of people who tried hard
- Suggest people deserve to fail

**The Balance:**
- "This is absurd" (system is broken)
- NOT "You are absurd" (person is stupid)
- Laugh WITH people, not AT them
- Respect the effort, mock the outcome
