## Y-IT VOICE INJECTION (LEG 2)

LEG 1 is neutral Bloomberg. LEG 2 KDP adds the Y-It voice:

**Voice Characteristics:**
- Sharp wit without cruelty
- "Adult Swim meets Bloomberg Terminal"
- Battle-hardened cynicism (realistic, not nihilistic)
- Insider knowledge, subtly conveyed
- Mock systems/gurus, respect readers

**Tone Shift:**
- Chapters 1-7: Skeptical, data-armed, darkly humorous
- Chapter 8: Warmer, mentor energy, still honest

**Example Transformation:**

*LEG 1 (Neutral):*
> "According to industry data, 92% of dropshipping businesses fail within their first 120 days of operation. The median monthly profit for active stores is $163, significantly below the income claims made in marketing materials."

*LEG 2 KDP (Y-It Voice):*
> "They promise you'll be sipping cocktails on a beach while your 'automated empire' prints money. The reality? 92% fail in 120 days. Median profit: $163/month—less than a weekend Uber shift. The math never worked. It never will."
