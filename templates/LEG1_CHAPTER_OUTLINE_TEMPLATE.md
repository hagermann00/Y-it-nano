## TEMPLATE OVERVIEW

LEG 1 is the "research dump" phase—maximum data density, exhaustive coverage, and academic neutrality. This content will be compressed 60-65% for KDP (7,800 words) and 40-45% for Web version (12,000+ words).

**Key Principles:**
- Lead with data, not opinion
- Include ALL relevant statistics (let LEG 2 cut)
- Maintain neutral, Bloomberg-report tone (let LEG 2 add Y-It voice)
- Over-document sources (easier to verify later)
- Include extended case studies (LEG 2 compresses)
