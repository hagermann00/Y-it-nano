### PORTRAIT 1: KAREN LEWIS - The Inventory Hoarder Who Hates It

**Visual Concept:** "What $5,200 in Poor Decisions Looks Like"

**Artistic Vision:**

- **Accent:** Aggressively red (#EF4444) - the spreadsheet that betrayed her
- **Background:** Beige warehouse neutral (#F5E6D3) - boring, underwhelming reality

**Composition Details:**
- Karen center, surrounded by gadget boxes
- One gadget in hand, pointing at it with comedic despair
- Boxes labeled with hilariously wrong predictions
- Spreadsheet in other hand, glowing comically red
- Expression: not tragic, just... annoyed at herself
- Lighting: unflattering bright warehouse light (comedic)
- Scale: she's TINY compared to the inventory tower

**Style Guidance:**
Hyper-detailed realistic photography, but framed like a comedy sketch. Not depressing—funny. The humor comes from the scale of the miscalculation. Think "hoarder intervention" but for failed businesses. The absurdity of owning 400 units of something nobody wants.

**Mood Markers:**
