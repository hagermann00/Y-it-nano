## RISK MITIGATION

### Top 5 Financial Risks

1. **Conversion stays at 3%** (probability: MEDIUM)
   - Mitigation: Funnel optimization (Issue #6), A/B testing, social proof
   - Contingency: Pivot to B2B/enterprise licensing if B2C fails

2. **Design quality inconsistent at $650/book** (probability: MEDIUM-HIGH)
   - Mitigation: Rework buffer (10%), detailed brief, first 3 books closely reviewed
   - Contingency: Upgrade to $1,500 designer after 10 books if needed

3. **24-month timeline too slow for market** (probability: LOW)
   - Mitigation: Focus on Tier 1 topics first, validate before expanding
   - Contingency: Hire 1 contract writer for Tier 1 topics only

4. **Operating costs exceed $2,270/month** (probability: MEDIUM)
   - Mitigation: AWS cost optimization, monitor usage, set billing alerts
   - Contingency: Reduce marketing spend, delay non-critical topics

5. **Amazon/Gumroad policy changes** (probability: LOW)
   - Mitigation: Diversify platforms (Stripe direct, Payhip, Lemon Squeezy)
   - Contingency: Pivot to direct sales via email list
