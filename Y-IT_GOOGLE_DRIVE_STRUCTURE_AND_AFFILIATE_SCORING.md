## 🎯 AFFILIATE OPPORTUNITIES SCORING SYSTEM

### Scoring Framework (10-Point Scale)

**File:** `AFFILIATE_SCORING_TEMPLATE.xlsx`

#### Scoring Criteria

| Criterion | Points | Definition |
|-----------|--------|-----------|
| **Commission Rate** | 0-2 | 2 pts: 30%+, 1 pt: 15-30%, 0 pts: <15% |
| **Conversion Probability** | 0-2 | 2 pts: >5%, 1 pt: 2-5%, 0 pts: <2% |
| **Integration Relevance** | 0-2 | 2 pts: Perfect fit, 1 pt: Good fit, 0 pts: Poor fit |
| **Brand Alignment** | 0-2 | 2 pts: Highly aligned, 1 pt: Neutral, 0 pts: Conflicting |
| **Approval Status** | 0-1 | 1 pt: Approved, 0 pts: Pending/Rejected |
| **Traffic Quality** | 0-1 | 1 pt: High quality, 0 pts: Low quality |

**Total:** 10-point scale
