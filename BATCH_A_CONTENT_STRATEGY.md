- CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost): $1,500-$3,000 per client

- Scaling paradox: Can't scale revenue without hiring, but hiring destroys margins

- Service ceiling: Solo operator maxes at 5-10 clients, then hits wall

**CHAPTER 6: The Failure Timeline (Why 90-95% Quit)**
