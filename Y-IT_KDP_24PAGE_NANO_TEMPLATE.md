## KDP TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS

### Print Specifications
- **Trim Size:** 6" × 9" (standard Amazon KDP)
- **Bleed:** 0.125" (3mm) on all sides
- **Page Count:** Exactly 24 pages (divisible by 4)
- **Paper Stock:** 60lb (90gsm) white or cream
- **Color:** Full color interior (4-color process)
- **DPI:** 300 minimum
- **File Format:** PDF/X-1a:2001 or PDF/X-3:2002
- **Color Profile:** CMYK, US Web Coated (SWOP) v2
- **Total Ink Coverage:** <240%

### Margins & Safe Areas
```
Inside margin (gutter):  0.75"
Outside margins:         0.5"
Top/Bottom margins:      0.5"
Safe area for text:      5" × 8"
Bleed extends to trim:   6.125" × 9.125"
```

### Cover File (Separate)
- **Dimensions:** 12.13" × 9.25" (includes front + spine + back)
- **Spine Width Calculator:** 24 pages × 0.0057" = 0.1368" ≈ 0.14" spine
- **Front Cover:** 6" × 9" (left side)
- **Spine:** 0.14" (center)
- **Back Cover:** 6" × 9" (right side)
- **Barcode Area:** 2" × 1.5" (bottom right, white background)

- Module openers: 1 full-width graphic (0.75" tall)
- Callout boxes: 2-3 per spread max
- Charts/graphs: 1-2 per module

- Icons: Used for data visualization
