- **Headers (H2):** Bebas Neue, 20pt, accent color
- **Body:** Lato, 11pt, dark gray (#333333)

- **Count:** 5-10 charts/graphs per book embedded within chapters
- **Format:** Vector graphics (SVG) preferred, high-res PNG acceptable
- **Colors:** Black/gray with accent color highlights

## CONTENT SPECIFICATIONS FOR DESIGNER

### Manuscript Structure (All 7 Books)
**Format:** 8 chapters, each with:
- Chapter opener (title page + opening statistic)
- 2-4 main sections per chapter
- 1-2 callout boxes per section
- 3-5 statistics/data points per chapter
- 2-3 case study references or embedded case studies
- Chapter closing paragraph with transition to next chapter

**Word Count Target:** 7,800 words final (compressed from ~20,000)
- Estimated: 180-220 pages per book at standard publishing format
- Assumption: ~35-45 words per page (with charts, white space)

### Case Studies Per Book
- **Total:** 3-4 detailed case studies per book (8 books = 24-28 total case studies)
- **Format:** 300-500 words each, timeline + financial impact included
- **Anonymization:** All names/identifying details changed
- **Placement:** Distributed across Chapters 2, 6, 7

### Statistics Integration
- **Per Chapter:** 3-5 key statistics/data points
- **Format:** Pulled out in callout boxes or bolded in text
- **Sources:** All statistics should include source attribution (2023-2025 data preferred)
- **Visual:** Some statistics should be represented as charts/graphs

### Affiliate Opportunities
- **Placement:** Chapter 7-8 (alternatives and "what works" sections)
- **Disclosure:** All affiliate links clearly marked with "affiliate link" notation
- **Diversity:** Mix of tools, platforms, courses, services
- **Commission Strategy:** 10-15% for tools, 20-30% for courses, 5-10% for services
