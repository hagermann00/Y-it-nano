# TRAP SHIPPING CASE STUDIES: PRINT VERSIONS (Compressed)
## 11 Failure Stories in 2-3 Lines Each

---

## CASE STUDY #1: The TikTok Flipper

**Alex the TikTok Flipper** - Watched viral "I made $1K in one week from Temu" video, bought 100 items for $120, listed on Etsy at 10x markup - 8 sales, 7 returns for "not as described," Etsy suspended account after 11 similar complaints from other buyers in same category

*Archetype: Trend Chaser | Loss: $370 | Timeline: 3 weeks | Age: 22*

---

## CASE STUDY #2: The Over-Financed Experimenter

**Marcus the Inheritance Winner** - $35K inheritance from grandmother, "I'll test 5 different business models simultaneously," spent $8K on Shopify + paid ads, $6K on Alibaba inventory, $5K on "coaching," paid VA $2K/month - After 6 months: 47 orders, $12K loss, realized he was paying consultants to manage his losses

*Archetype: Over-Financed Experimenter | Loss: $27,000+ | Timeline: 24 weeks | Age: 31*

---

## CASE STUDY #3: The Fulfillment Failure

**Jennifer the VA Manager** - Built beautiful Shopify store, got 200 clicks from $800 ad spend, first 6 orders arrived damaged (supplier error), 5 initiated chargebacks, Jennifer had zero recourse with supplier - Realized she was managing customer anger while supplier (5,000 miles away) didn't care about quality

*Archetype: Fulfillment Failure | Loss: $1,200 | Timeline: 6 weeks | Age: 28*

---

## CASE STUDY #4: The Platform Manipulator

**David the Internet Marketer** - "Gamed" Amazon algorithm with 47 fake 5-star reviews from Fiverr, drove traffic via SEO manipulation tactics - Sold $15K worth in 3 weeks, then Amazon permanently suspended account, froze $8K in pending payments, blacklisted email/payment method

*Archetype: Platform Manipulator | Loss: $8,000+ (frozen funds) | Timeline: 3 weeks | Age: 25*

---

## CASE STUDY #5: The Niche Expert Misfit

**Robert the Fashion Expert** - 15 years as retail buyer, knew "quality women's accessories," started curating "authentic" items from Alibaba to position against cheap competitors - Bought $4,200 inventory, sold 16 items in 3 months, realized "authentic" doesn't matter when competitors offer same products 60% cheaper; customers price-shopped, not quality-shopped

*Archetype: Niche Expert | Loss: $4,000 | Timeline: 12 weeks | Age: 45*

---

## CASE STUDY #6: The Paid Ad Dependent

**Sophia the LinkedIn Seller** - Spent $5,200 on Facebook + TikTok ads driving to trap shipping store, achieved 2,800 clicks, 340 site visitors, 12 orders (~0.35% conversion) - Each order cost $435 in ad spend, products had 40% return rate - Realized she was paying $600+ per viable customer; walked away

*Archetype: Paid Ad Dependent | Loss: $5,200+ | Timeline: 8 weeks | Age: 29*

---

## CASE STUDY #7: The Automation Trap

**Tyler the Developer** - Built custom Python bot to source products from AliExpress, auto-list on multiple platforms, managed inventory sync, handled customer service templates - Spent 180 hours coding, $400 in tools/hosting - After 3 months: 0 orders, realized perfect automation of a broken business model = faster failure

*Archetype: Tech Optimizer | Loss: $400 + opportunity cost | Timeline: 12 weeks | Age: 26*

---

## CASE STUDY #8: The Overnight Success Believer

**Patricia the Corporate Escape Artist** - 47, burned out from 20 years in finance, "I'll hire a VA to run this," spent $6K on inventory + $1,200/month on VA to "manage everything" - After 8 weeks, VA was managing returns/chargebacks/refunds but zero new sales; Patricia realized "passive" doesn't mean "unmanaged"

*Archetype: Overnight Success Believer | Loss: $8,400 | Timeline: 12 weeks | Age: 47*

---

## CASE STUDY #9: The Multi-Platform Scatterer

**Brian the Multi-Platform Optimizer** - "Diversification strategy" = buying same inventory, listing on Amazon FBA + Etsy + eBay + Shopify simultaneously - 10% conversion on Amazon, 4% on Etsy, 2% on eBay, 1% on Shopify, managed returns across all four, gave 80% attention to 20% of channels - After 4 months: lost $3,400, made 0.5 hours per day on all platforms

*Archetype: Multi-Platform Scatterer | Loss: $3,400 | Timeline: 16 weeks | Age: 34*

---

## CASE STUDY #10: The Side Hustler Burnout

**Luis the Overcommitted** - Full-time job, 2 kids, side gig as Uber driver, "I'll add trap shipping," managed 6 hours/week for 8 weeks, returned 18 customer emails unanswered, got 9 chargebacks for "no communication," platform suspended for unresponsiveness - Realized "side hustle" doesn't work when side business needs full-time attention

*Archetype: Side Hustler | Loss: $1,800 | Timeline: 8 weeks | Age: 38*

---

## CASE STUDY #11: The Passion Play

**Emma the Obsessive Seller** - "I'm OBSESSED with sustainable home goods," spent $3K curating "high-quality eco-friendly" items from Alibaba - Customers loved the IDEA but purchased from competitors 40% cheaper, Amazon/Temu - Emma's blog posts about sustainability built great audience (5K followers) but zero conversion to orders (followers weren't buyers; they were doomscrollers)

*Archetype: Passion-Driven | Loss: $3,000 | Timeline: 12 weeks | Age: 31*

## THE PATTERN ACROSS ALL ARCHETYPES

1. **The Promise**: "This is easy, passive, proven by others"
2. **The Belief**: "I can replicate this with minimal additional effort"
3. **The Reality**: Operational complexity, customer service demands, supplier failures
4. **The Failure**: Returns, chargebacks, platform suspensions, or simple lack of sales
5. **The Cost**: Money ($370-$27K), time (3-24 weeks), and lost opportunity

**Success Rate Across All Archetypes: 0 out of 11 (0%)**

This mirrors the real-world success rate for trap shipping: 2-5% (meaning 95-98% fail).
