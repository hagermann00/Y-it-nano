## LOCKED DECISIONS (FINAL)

### Format & Structure
- **Page Format:** 6×9" trade paperback (KDP standard)
- **Page Count:** 24 pages (14 core + 10 addendum)
- **Word Count:** 7,800 words (4,500 core + 2,900 addendum)
- **Case Studies:** All 11 compressed to 2-3 lines in print (full versions on web)
- **Images:** 8-10 custom illustrations (all specified)
- **Title Page:** Hero image + QR code (animated comic on web)

### Voice & Tone (CRITICAL)
- **Core Voice:** Satirical contrarian analyst with intellectual jujitsu
- **Tone:** Sharp wit without cruelty, intellectual superiority without condescension
- **Credibility:** Insider knowledge implied, never explicit bragging
- **Chapters 1-7:** Constant satirical edge, battle-hardened cynicism
- **Chapter 8:** Warmer, hopeful, earned optimism
- **Forbidden:** Generic motivation, unsubstantiated claims, fluff, guru tactics

### Economics & Pricing
- **Print Price:** $2.99 KDP
- **Print Margin:** $0.60-0.75 per copy (viable at 500+/month)
- **Bundle Options:** Print + Digital ($5.99), All Formats ($12.99)
- **Web Premium:** $7.99 one-time / $4.99/month
- **Annual Subscription:** $99/year (all 7 books, all formats)

### Design Specifications
- **Colors:** Exact hex codes (primary, accent, warning, success colors)
- **Typography:** Single sans-serif family, 11pt body, justified, 1.2 line height
- **Images:** 300 DPI minimum, CMYK, KDP compliant
- **Safety Zone:** 0.25" from trim edges (critical content only)
- **Bleed:** 0.125" on all sides
- **Margins:** 0.75" top/bottom, 0.625" inside, 0.5" outside

## INTEGRATION HIERARCHY (When Guidelines Conflict)

**Priority Order (1 = highest, 5 = lowest):**

1. **KDP Technical Requirements** (Non-negotiable)
   - Page dimensions: 6.0" × 9.0" trim, 6.25" × 9.25" with bleed
   - Resolution: 300 DPI minimum for all images
   - Safety zone: 0.25" from trim edge
   - Margins: Exact measurements as specified
   - PDF format: PDF/X-1a:2001 or PDF/X-3:2002

2. **Color Palette Adherence** (Exact hex codes, no substitutions)
   - Background: #F5F2EC primary, #E5E0D8 secondary
   - Text: #3A3A3A primary, #2E2E2E secondary
   - Accent: #5D7BEA blue, #E35050 red, #6EC972 green
   - No exceptions, no approximations

3. **Character Consistency** (Chad & PosiBot must match across chapters)
   - Same design, same proportions, same expressions
   - Chad shows deterioration Chapters 1-7, recovery Chapter 8
   - PosiBot unchanging optimistic throughout
   - Reference previous chapters when generating new content

4. **Typography System** (Font family, sizing, hierarchy, KDP formatting)
   - Font: Single sans-serif family (Helvetica, Arial, or Open Sans)
   - Body: 11pt, justified, 1.2 line height, 6pt spacing
   - Hierarchy: 24pt titles, 18pt headers, 14pt subheaders
   - Consistency: Same family throughout, no mixing

5. **Tone & Voice** (Satirical edge, insider credibility, appropriate chapter tone)
   - Chapters 1-7: Cynical, data-driven, mocking
   - Chapter 8: Hopeful, constructive, supportive
   - Always: Evidence-based, never fluffy, never guru-like
