## ⚠️ WEEK 1 RISK MITIGATION

### Risk: Designer Not Available or Doesn't Work Out
**Probability:** Medium | **Impact:** +2 week delay
**Mitigation:**
- Have 2 backup designers identified before offering contract
- Use Canva templates as fallback for Batch A (faster iteration)
- Founder can do basic layout if needed (slower but maintains schedule)

### Risk: Infrastructure Issues Delay Developer
**Probability:** Medium | **Impact:** 1-3 days delay
**Mitigation:**
- Use Vercel (no infrastructure complexity)
- Use Supabase (managed PostgreSQL, no setup needed)
- Pre-test all tools before Week 0

### Risk: Research Data Gaps Delay Topics
**Probability:** Medium | **Impact:** 1-2 day delay per topic
**Mitigation:**
- Use 60% existing data (Dropshipping example)
- Have secondary source list ready
- Accept "good enough" over perfect research
- AI agents help fill gaps quickly

### Risk: Contractor Communication Breaks Down
**Probability:** Low | **Impact:** 2-3 week delay
**Mitigation:**
- Daily standups (15 min sync)
- Shared tracking (GitHub, Asana)
- Weekly video calls with designer and developer
- Clear written specs and approval workflows

### Risk: Founder Gets Bottlenecked on Reviews
**Probability:** High | **Impact:** Delays all downstream work
**Mitigation:**
- Set review SLA: 24 hours max turnaround
- Use async review (written feedback, not meetings)
- Empower designer to make judgment calls
- Batch reviews (e.g., all Mon/Wed/Fri)
