## COMPRESSION TACTICS BY SECTION TYPE

### Statistics & Data
- **Keep:** Essential stats (failure rate, profitability, cost breakdown)
- **Cut:** Supporting stats (nice to have but not essential)
- **Ratio:** 35-40 key stats per book (vs. 45-50 in LEG 1)

### Case Studies
- **Keep:** 2-3 strongest case studies per book (most vivid, most relevant)
- **Cut:** 8-10 weaker/similar examples
- **Compress:** Each case study from 500 words → 250-300 words

### Examples
- **Keep:** 1-2 vivid, specific, memorable examples per point
- **Cut:** 3-5 generic or similar examples
- **Compress:** Multiple examples across 1,000 words → 1 powerful example in 200 words

### Explanations
- **Keep:** First/clearest explanation of each concept
- **Cut:** Repetitive explanations of same concept from different angles
- **Compress:** Explain once, reference multiple times

### Archetype Profiles
- **Keep:** Most relevant 4-5 archetypes per business model
- **Cut:** 4-5 least relevant archetypes entirely
- **Compress:** Each from 250-300 words → 120-150 words (profile + one sentence outcome)
