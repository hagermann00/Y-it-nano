## THE PROMPT

You are a business intelligence researcher conducting comprehensive analysis on **[BUSINESS_TOPIC]**. Execute a systematic, multi-dimensional research investigation covering all critical business aspects. Use current data sources, authoritative industry reports, and real-world evidence.

### RESEARCH EXECUTION FRAMEWORK

**PHASE 1: MARKET LANDSCAPE INTELLIGENCE**
- Market size, growth rates, and revenue projections (current and 5-year forecast)
- Key market drivers, trends, and disruption factors
- Regulatory environment and compliance requirements
- Geographic market variations and opportunities
- Market maturity stage and lifecycle position
- Economic factors affecting market dynamics

**PHASE 2: CUSTOMER INTELLIGENCE PROFILE**
- Primary target demographic and psychographic profiles
- Customer pain points, needs, and unmet demands
- Buying behavior patterns and decision-making process
- Price sensitivity and willingness to pay analysis
- Customer acquisition channels and preferences
- Retention challenges and loyalty factors
- B2B vs B2C considerations (where applicable)

**PHASE 3: PRODUCT/SERVICE ECOSYSTEM ANALYSIS**
- Core offerings and service categories within the space
- Feature sets, capabilities, and differentiation factors
- Pricing models and revenue structures
- Technology requirements and infrastructure needs
- Supply chain considerations and dependencies
- Quality standards and performance benchmarks
- Innovation trends and emerging capabilities

**PHASE 4: COMPETITIVE LANDSCAPE MAPPING**
- Direct competitors (top 5-10 players) with market share data
- Indirect competitors and substitute solutions
- Competitive strengths, weaknesses, and positioning
- Pricing strategies and competitive advantages
- Market entry barriers and competitive moats
- Recent competitive moves, acquisitions, and strategic shifts
- White space opportunities and underserved segments

**PHASE 5: OPERATIONAL INTELLIGENCE**
- Business model variations and success patterns
- Key success factors and failure points
- Resource requirements (capital, human, technology)
- Operational challenges and scalability factors
- Partnership opportunities and ecosystem players
- Distribution channels and go-to-market strategies

**PHASE 6: RISK AND OPPORTUNITY ASSESSMENT**
- Primary business risks and mitigation strategies
- Market threats and defensive positioning
- Growth opportunities and expansion pathways
- Technology risks and innovation requirements
- Financial risks and capital considerations
- Regulatory and compliance risks

**PHASE 7: STRATEGIC SYNTHESIS AND ACTIONABLE INSIGHTS**
- Market attractiveness score with supporting rationale
- Competitive positioning recommendations
- Entry strategy options with pros/cons analysis
- Investment requirements and ROI projections
- Critical success factors for market entry
- Timeline considerations and milestone mapping
- Risk mitigation priorities and contingency planning

### OUTPUT REQUIREMENTS

**STRUCTURE YOUR RESPONSE AS:**

1. **EXECUTIVE SUMMARY** (300-500 words)
   - Market opportunity assessment
   - Key findings and strategic recommendations
   - Risk/reward profile

2. **DETAILED RESEARCH FINDINGS** (organized by the 7 phases above)
   - Use data, statistics, and specific examples
   - Include company names, market figures, and concrete evidence
   - Cite sources and provide context

3. **STRATEGIC RECOMMENDATIONS** (actionable insights)
   - Specific next steps and strategic options
   - Investment and resource recommendations
   - Timeline and priority matrix

4. **RESEARCH SOURCES AND METHODOLOGY**
   - List of primary sources used
   - Data collection methodology notes
   - Reliability assessment of key findings

### RESEARCH STANDARDS
- Use current data (2023-2025 preferred, 2022 minimum)
- Include specific numbers, percentages, and quantitative metrics
- Reference authoritative sources (industry reports, financial filings, market research)
- Provide company examples and real-world case studies
- Cross-reference findings across multiple sources
- Identify data gaps and research limitations

### CRITICAL SUCCESS CRITERIA
Your research must be:
- **COMPREHENSIVE**: Cover all 7 research phases thoroughly
- **CURRENT**: Use recent data and reflect current market conditions
- **SPECIFIC**: Include concrete examples, numbers, and company references
- **ACTIONABLE**: Provide clear strategic recommendations and next steps
- **EVIDENCE-BASED**: Support all claims with credible sources and data

Execute this research with the thoroughness of a professional business intelligence report. Leave no critical business dimension unexplored.

## IMPLEMENTATION GUIDE FOR Y-It PROJECTS

### Usage for Each Topic
For each of the 50 Y-It nano-books, apply this framework as follows:

**Step 1: Define Topic**
- Substitute [BUSINESS_TOPIC] with specific topic (e.g., "Dropshipping as a Business Model")

**Step 2: Execute Research**
- Use the 7-phase framework systematically
- Gather current data (2023-2025 sources)
- Cross-reference multiple authoritative sources
- Document failure rates, costs, timelines, and barriers

**Step 3: Validate Content**
- Cross-check existing manuscript against research findings
- Verify all statistics and case study claims
- Update outdated information
- Fill research gaps with current data

**Step 4: Build Case Studies**
- Use Phase 4-6 findings to develop realistic failure archetypes
- Ground case studies in actual market data
- Create anonymized composites based on real patterns

**Step 5: Quality Gate**
- Ensure all claims have documented sources
- Verify failure rates against multiple sources
- Confirm alternatives recommendations have market evidence
- Approve for publication when research is comprehensive
